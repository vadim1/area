Define Success

By starting your decision making where you want to end up you can make big decisions better.

Instead of open-ended problem solving answering the question: "What am I solving for?" enables you to define your Critical Concepts.

Critical Concepts are the one, two or three things that need to happen in the outcome of your decision for you to know that the decision has succeeded for you personally.
