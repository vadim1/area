Introduction to the Game

We make thousands of decisions every day - from when to wake up to whether to have a late night snack - and we make them quickly, and largely unconsciously. But what happens when we face a high stakes decision? Knowing your PROBLEM SOLVER PROFILE will help you learn about your decision-making strengths and your potential blind spots.

The PROBLEM SOLVER PROFILE is not prospective, it tells you what you tend to do when making decisions. By learning more about you as a decision maker you can make big decisions better.
