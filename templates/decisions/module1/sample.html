Take a question like "Should I go away to college?" This has no right answer but it probably has a right answer for YOU.

Instead of answering that question which may be difficult AREA inverts decision making to focus on your vision of success.

Answer this: How would you know that you've made a good decision for you? List three things.

Let's take Nylah for example. She's a senior living in an apartment in Brooklyn with her mom and dad and two younger sisters.

When she uses the AREA Method and asks herself how she'd know that she's made a good decision about which college to go to she realizes that three things have to happen for the decision to succeed.

Nylah's question: "How will I know that I've made a good decision?"
